## Design Context

### Users
METIS is for privacy-conscious knowledge workers, researchers, and builders who want a local-first AI workspace that runs on their own machine. They use the landing page as a living workspace surface to pull documents into orbit, shape a constellation of capabilities, and move quickly into grounded chat.

### Brand Personality
Private, expansive, cerebral. The product should feel intelligent and exploratory without becoming noisy or game-like. Motion should suggest a living cosmos and growing cognition, while still feeling calm and trustworthy.

### Aesthetic Direction
The landing page is a deep-space constellation rather than a dashboard. Stars, faculties, and observatories should feel like parts of one coherent celestial system. Use cosmic scale, soft atmospheric light, and meaningful color drawn from the faculty model. Avoid generic neon cyberpunk, decorative glass overload, or playful toy-like space themes.

### Design Principles
1. Keep the constellation metaphor functional: every visual flourish should help the user understand growth, influence, or proximity.
2. Preserve a calm, premium rhythm: expansive scale is good, clutter and frantic motion are not.
3. Let faculty color carry meaning: connected stars should visibly inherit and blend the influences they draw from.
4. Make wonder usable: large-scale cosmic interactions should stay discoverable, performant, and grounded in clear controls.
5. Respect accessibility and device limits: reduced motion must remain supported, and the landing canvas should stay smooth on a typical laptop.
